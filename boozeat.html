    Boozeat.com was established in Malaysia since 2015. Due to the increasing volume of orders, its website needed to be enhanced to cope with the demand. However, upgrading the existing platform would time-consuming. We saw this as an opportunity to not only build a new platform from ground up, but also improve the shopping experience with new ideas, especially in a market where customers demand quick turnaround for purchases and delivery.

  • The challenge

    It’s not feasible to expand the existing website platform as the product handling and processing of orders took a long time. We interviewed selected target audiences and found more user problems to address.

  • The process

    To build an entirely new, customisable platform with a more seamless shopping experience. These are the UX stages or steps involved.

    • Early brainstorming and discussion on MVP and direction
    • User research and website insights analysis
    • Mockup concepts and tone of voice for UX writing
    • High-fidelity prototyping (Invision)
    • Building the filter navigation and product categories (card sorting)
    • UIUX of the admin dashboard
    • Handover of design to development
    • Created website banner templates and guidelines for client’s internal team

  • The website

    To make the website more appealing, we came up with a trendy design, streamlined navigation, simplified user journey to checkout page, and injected cheeky yet compelling copy, all of which makes the process of ordering on the website more seamless.

  • The results

    Online sales increase 3 fold in the span of 3 months.
